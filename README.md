# Myntra Social Media Intelligence & Campaign Analysis (Excel Project)

## Project Overview
Myntra is one of India’s leading fashion e-commerce platforms with a strong presence across
Instagram, Facebook, Twitter (X), and YouTube. With increasing investments in influencer
marketing, reels, videos, and large-scale campaigns such as **End of Reason Sale (EORS)** and
**Big Fashion Festival (BFF)**, the marketing team needed clear, data-backed insights to connect
social media activity with measurable business outcomes.

This project builds a **complete Social Media Intelligence Solution using Microsoft Excel**
to analyze Myntra’s social media performance, campaign effectiveness, content strategy,
and platform-wise ROI.

---

## Business Objectives
- Boost brand engagement among fashion-conscious users  
- Educate customers about new fashion trends and collections  
- Strengthen customer loyalty through consistent engagement  
- Maximize ROI from social media ad spend  
- Identify high-performing content formats and campaigns  

---

## Datasets Used
### 1. Posts Dataset
Contains post-level data across platforms for engagement and content analysis.  
**Key fields:**  
Post_ID, Platform, Date, Content_Type, Likes, Shares, Comments, Impressions, Reach,
Clicks, Hashtags_Used, Campaign_Name

### 2. Engagement Summary Dataset
Aggregated weekly/monthly platform performance data.  
**Key fields:**  
Week/Month, Platform, Total_Posts, Avg_Likes, Avg_Shares, Avg_Comments,
Total_Impressions, Total_Reach, Engagement_Rate

### 3. Campaign Metadata Dataset
Campaign-level performance and ROI metrics.  
**Key fields:**  
Campaign_Name, Start_Date, End_Date, Platforms_Used, Total_Impressions,
Total_Engagement, Campaign_Goal, ROI_Metrics

## Key Tasks Performed

### 1. Data Preprocessing & Cleaning
- Removed duplicate post records  
- Standardized date formats and platform names  
- Corrected numeric fields (likes, impressions, clicks, ad spend)  
- Split hashtags into individual tags for analysis  

### 2. Engagement Analysis
- Calculated engagement rate per post  
- Identified Top 10 highest-engagement posts  
- Created pivot tables for:
  - Engagement by content type & platform  
  - Average clicks per hashtag  
- Identified top-performing hashtags  

### 3. Platform Analysis
- Compared engagement across Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, and Twitter  
- Analyzed follower growth trends per platform  
- Visualized Engagement vs Ad Spend  
- Evaluated platform focus vs multi-platform strategy  

### 4. Hashtag & Content Strategy
- Identified most frequently used hashtags  
- Compared engagement performance by hashtag  
- Analyzed content types (Videos, Images, Carousels)  
- Recommended platform-wise content priorities  

### 5. Campaign Effectiveness
- Calculated total & average impressions, likes, and clicks per campaign  
- Measured engagement uplift during campaigns  
- Identified highest ROI campaigns  
- Analyzed follower growth impact  

### 6. Follower Retention & Loyalty
- Visualized weekly follower growth using line charts  
- Identified peak growth periods  
- Applied moving averages to smooth trends  
- Correlated ad spend with follower growth  

---

## Key Insights
- Video and Reel-based content generated the highest engagement across platforms  
- Instagram and YouTube delivered the strongest ROI for fashion-focused campaigns  
- Sales-driven campaigns showed clear spikes in engagement and follower growth  
- Hashtag optimization significantly improved reach and click-through rates  

---

## Recommendations
- Prioritize video and reel content for Instagram and YouTube  
- Allocate higher ad spend during major sale campaigns  
- Focus on consistent hashtag strategies to improve discoverability  
- Maintain a multi-platform presence while prioritizing high-ROI platforms  

---

## Project Outcome
This Excel-based solution empowers Myntra’s marketing team to:
- Make data-driven campaign decisions  
- Optimize content strategy by platform  
- Improve engagement, follower growth, and ROI
